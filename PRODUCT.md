# PRODUCT.md — Inside the Billion Dollar Mind

## Register
Brand (experience site). Design IS the product: the site is a walkable "world"
version of the book The Billion Dollar Mind. Marketing-register rules apply,
but reading comfort outranks spectacle inside the rooms.

## Who it's for
Buyers of the book: business owners, mostly NON-technical, arriving via a QR
code on a printed page. They came to get one chapter's thinking trained into
them and to run one AI coach prompt. Many have never pasted a prompt into an
AI tool before.

## What it does
Seven rooms, one per chapter. Each room: the principle, Jay's way of seeing
it, two true stories, three diagnostic checks, and a copy-paste AI coach
prompt (Claude / ChatGPT / Jay-I). An email gate (Ticket Check) unlocks the
prompts. The /world page is a cinematic scroll fly-through used as the front
door and demo.

## Brand personality
"Decoding Genius" black and gold: pitch-black museum interiors, warm gold
volumetric light, monospace labels, cinematic restraint. Dignified, curated,
a private archive after hours. Never SaaS-bright, never busy.

## Strategic design principles
1. One goal per room: reader leaves with the prompt run or copied.
2. Progressive disclosure over walls of text: short first serving, "read
   more" to go deeper. Non-technical readers must never feel tested.
3. The reader chooses depth (quick start / guided / route-me), never forced.
4. Clone-agnostic: prompts are artifacts that run anywhere; no platform
   lock-in language.
5. Locked visual system: existing black/gold tokens and mono labels win over
   generic best practice (identity preservation).

## Anti-references
Cream/beige AI-default pages; SaaS dashboards; crypto-dark neon; anything
that reads "template". Long unbroken prose columns.
